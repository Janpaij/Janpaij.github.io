About Us

Since 2006 when Bright Futures was founded, we have consistently provided our students with the highest quality of teaching experience catered to individual needs. We are proud to say that our core value is to give utmost care and attention to each student - focusing on both thorough understanding and academic attainment, as our passion is that each student will reach their full potential.

Initially we provided tutoring for the 11+ examinations, responding to the success and demand for our services, we expanded and now cover every level of the UK education system. This includes:

  • Maths and Science subjects at all primary & secondary school levels
  • 11+ examinations
  • All Mathematics and Science modules at GCSE, A-level and higher education level
  • University entrance examinations

We have developed a thorough understanding of the teaching and examination systems over many years and cater to different boards and levels of the students. We are very proud of the successes of our students through the years and look forward to seeing many more.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can multiple subjects be taught in one session?

    Yes, it is possible to combine more than one subjects in a given lesson. For example, during a two hour session, the first hour can be dedicated to Physics, and the second hour Chemistry. We are flexible in this regard. Please contact us to discuss any specific enquiries.

  • How long does each lesson last?

    Generally the length of a lesson will depend on the subject or subjects being taught, and the level to which they are being taught. We can be flexible to accomodate the specific needs of each student. Please contact us to discuss further.

  • Do Bright Futures hold lessons during evenings/weekends/holidays?

    Yes, we understand that our students are often tied to multiple commitments so may need early sessions or evening sessions and that they often like to utilise term time breaks to get ahead, so we are as flexible as possible according to their schedules.

  • How can Bright Futures help with exam preparations?

    We are very experienced in terms of typical exam content and formats across various boards, and have access to a variety of resources such as mock exams. We offer going through common questions and can guide our students through exam solutions to put them in best stead for the exams themselves.

  • How will Bright Futures cater to my learning needs?

    At Bright Futures, we remain open to varying stages and aims of our student, and will discuss these before starting sessions. We would always be guided by this rather than a one size fits all approach.

  • How many students per session?

    With our highly individualised approach, we usually teach one to one or in small groups (2-4 students).

  • How often may we have sessions?

    Currently almost all of our students attend sessions on a weekly basis. However we recognise that students will have different preferences for frequency of sessions and that needs will change through the course of the academic year. The timetable remains flexible rather than fixed to accommodate this, sessions may take place weekly or be adjusted to more or less frequently as needed.
